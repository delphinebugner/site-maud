body_en: Beethoven’s \*Missa solemnis\* for soloists, choir and orchestra was
  composed between 1818 and 1823 and premiered in its entirety on 7 April 1824.
  The interplay between the voices and the orchestra, together with an
  exceptionally rich instrumentation, lend this solemn mass – which Beethoven
  regarded as his most accomplished work – an unprecedented dramatic intensity.
  It will be brought to life by the sounds of period instruments played by the
  Cercle de l’Harmonie, conducted by Jérémie Rhorer.
